## 📌 Overview

> End-to-end analysis of COVID-19 global data covering **10 countries** from **March 2020 to December 2023**.  
> The project explores infection rates, mortality trends, and vaccination progress using rich time-series visualizations and a concise insight report.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze cumulative COVID-19 cases and deaths across countries
- Track vaccination rollout and its impact on mortality
- Identify wave patterns in weekly new case data
- Compare countries by cases per million and deaths per million
- Build a mortality rate heatmap across years

---

## 🌍 Countries Covered

| 🇺🇸 USA | 🇮🇳 India | 🇧🇷 Brazil | 🇬🇧 UK | 🇩🇪 Germany |
|--------|---------|---------|------|---------|
| 🇫🇷 France | 🇪🇬 Egypt | 🇮🇹 Italy | 🇷🇺 Russia | 🇯🇵 Japan |

---

## 📊 Key Visualizations

| Chart | Insight |
|-------|---------|
| 📈 Cumulative Cases Over Time | Clear wave patterns across 2020–2023 |
| 💀 Deaths per Million by Country | Significant variation between nations |
| 💉 Vaccination Progress | Japan & UK lead vaccination rates |
| 🔥 Mortality Rate Heatmap | Mortality declined sharply post-vaccination |
| 📉 Weekly New Cases Trend | Winter peaks visible across all years |
| 🔵 Vaccination vs Cases Scatter | Higher vax rate = lower case burden |

---

## 💡 Key Findings

- 💉 Countries with **higher vaccination rates** show significantly lower mortality in 2022–2023
- 📉 **Mortality rates** declined across all countries as vaccines rolled out in 2021
- 🌊 Clear **wave patterns** peak in Winter months every year
- 🇯🇵 **Japan** achieved the highest vaccination rate with the lowest mortality
- 🇪🇬 **Egypt** had the lowest vaccination rate among analyzed countries
